About Us

We are a woman-owned food business driven by family recipes and a passion for all things flavorful, serving authentic and innovative cuisine in mid-Michigan. In addition to our Latin Thai fusion food truck, we also create small-batch artisan tortilla chips & salsa. Stop by one of our partner stores to try Urbano's for yourself, and get in touch to book us for your next event!

Central Fusion Food Truck

Blending recipes from our roots, we create unique Latin & Thai fusion dishes that are made to order with bold flavors and the freshest ingredients. Find us in Mt. Pleasant or contact us to bring a delicious culinary experience to you!


Urbano's Chips & Salsa


Perfected over years as a family recipe, Urbano's Tortilla Chips offer a craveable "thick & crispy" texture, perfect for holding lots of our fresh salsa. Experience the superior taste for yourself and see why our fans can't get enough!
